Key Differences Explained

Price and Business Model

OffRecord AI Journal and Apple Journal are the only two apps that are truly free with no paid tiers. Most alternatives gate important features behind subscriptions ranging from $23.99 to $96 per year. Reflectly is the most expensive at $59.99/yr with no free tier for core features.

Privacy

Only OffRecord AI Journal and Apple Journal carry Apple's "Data Not Collected" privacy label. Every other app in this comparison collects some combination of identifiers, usage data, diagnostics, or purchase history. For journaling — one of the most personal activities you can do on your phone — this matters significantly.

AI and Intelligence

OffRecord AI Journal is the only app that offers a full on-device AI system including a Friday, mood prediction, personality cards, and weekly insights — all running without any cloud dependency. Journey, Reflectly, and Notion offer AI features, but these require sending your data to cloud servers for processing.

Voice Journaling

OffRecord AI Journal is the only app with full on-device voice-to-text journaling. Day One supports audio attachments but does not transcribe them. Journey offers limited voice input through cloud processing. The remaining apps have no voice journaling capabilities.

Data Portability

OffRecord AI Journal offers the most export formats: PDF, JSON, Markdown, CSV, plain text, and AES-256 encrypted backups. Apple Journal and Reflectly offer no export at all, which means your data is effectively locked in. This is worth considering before committing to a journaling habit in any app.
