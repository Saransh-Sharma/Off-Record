Best Penzu Alternative (2026): Free Private Journal

April 12, 2026 · 5 min read

Penzu was one of the first online journals to emphasize privacy and security. The "lock" metaphor — a private journal behind a password — resonated with people who wanted a digital diary that felt safe. But the app hasn't evolved much, and if you're looking for something more modern, OffRecord AI Journal offers a fundamentally different approach to private journaling.

What Penzu Does Well

Penzu's focus on privacy was ahead of its time. The military-grade encryption, custom lock screens, and emphasis on personal security gave users confidence that their words were protected. The writing interface is clean and distraction-free, and the customizable journal covers add a personal touch.

Where Penzu Falls Short

Cloud-based storage: Despite marketing itself as "private," Penzu stores your journal data on their servers. Encrypted, yes — but your most personal writing still lives on someone else's computer. True privacy means your data never leaves your device.

Subscription pricing: Penzu Pro costs $49.99/year. That's one of the more expensive journal subscriptions on the market. The free tier is very limited.

Dated interface: Penzu's interface hasn't kept pace with modern iOS design. It feels like an app from 2015, not 2026.

No AI features: Penzu offers no mood tracking, no sentiment analysis, no pattern detection. It's a text editor with a lock on it.

Text-only: No voice input. If you want to journal without typing, Penzu doesn't offer that option.

How OffRecord AI Journal Compares

True on-device privacy: OffRecord AI Journal doesn't store anything on servers because there are no servers. Everything lives on your iPhone. No account. No cloud. Apple's privacy label reads "Data Not Collected." This is a fundamentally stronger privacy model than encrypted cloud storage.

Voice-first: Speak instead of type. Two minutes of talking. OffRecord AI Journal transcribes on-device. Perfect for people who want to journal but hate the blank page.

On-device AI: Mood tracking, sentiment analysis, and pattern detection — all running locally on your iPhone. Penzu offers none of this.

Free: No subscription. No premium tier. Every feature available at no cost.

What OffRecord AI Journal Doesn't Have

OffRecord AI Journal doesn't have a web interface, custom journal covers, or the ability to create multiple separate journals. If you need web access to your journal or want a more traditional writing experience, those are trade-offs.

Who Should Switch

Consider OffRecord AI Journal if you: chose Penzu for privacy but want truly on-device storage; want AI features like mood tracking; prefer speaking to typing; or don't want to pay $50/year for a journal.
