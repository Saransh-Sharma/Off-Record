Privacy as architecture

Your journal is not a dataset. It is yours.

Most AI journal apps ask you to trust their cloud. OffRecord takes the opposite path: the intelligence lives with the data, on the device in your hand.

  • No account required. Start journaling without an email address, login, or profile.
  • No analytics or ad tracking. The app is built without third-party analytics, advertising, or telemetry SDKs.
  • On-device AI. Speech, sentiment, entities, insights, and Friday processing run locally.
  • Optional iCloud sync. Sync through your personal Apple account only when you choose it.
