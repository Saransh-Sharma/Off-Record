April 12, 2026

You talk for a living, but always for an audience. Voice journaling is talking without an audience — the unedited, unpublished version of your thoughts.

Why Voice Journaling Works for Podcasters

You speak 150 words per minute. You type 40. For podcasters who are already exhausted at the end of the day, the 2-minute voice journal is the only journaling method that sticks. No typing, no blank page, no time commitment.

OffRecord AI Journal transcribes your voice on-device, detects your mood automatically, and lets Friday learn your personality patterns over time. Everything stays on your phone — no servers, no cloud, no account required.

How to Start

Open OffRecord AI Journal between episodes. Press record. Talk for 2 minutes about your day. That's it. The AI handles mood detection, keyword extraction, and personality modeling. You just talk.
