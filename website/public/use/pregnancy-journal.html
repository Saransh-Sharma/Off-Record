Voice Journal Your Pregnancy: Hands-Free Baby Diary

April 12, 2026 · 4 min read

Pregnancy is a nine-month experience you'll want to remember but will definitely forget the details of. The nausea of the first trimester, the first kick, the strange dreams, the anxiety before the anatomy scan, the nesting phase, the moment it all became real. Every parent says the same thing afterward: "I wish I'd documented it more."

But during pregnancy, you're exhausted. Writing in a journal requires energy you don't have — especially in the first and third trimesters. Voice journaling lets you capture the experience without the effort.

Speak Your Pregnancy Story

With OffRecord AI Journal, you can journal about your pregnancy in two minutes. Speak about how you feel today. What the baby is doing. What you're excited about. What scares you. The honest stuff you don't post on social media — the discomfort, the ambivalence, the overwhelm alongside the joy.

OffRecord AI Journal transcribes everything on-device and tracks your emotional patterns. Over nine months, you'll build a rich, honest record of one of the most transformative experiences of your life.

Track Mood Across Trimesters

Pregnancy hormones create emotional swings that can be disorienting. OffRecord AI Journal's on-device AI identifies mood patterns across your entries. You'll see how your emotional landscape shifts across trimesters, which weeks are hardest, and whether your anxiety spikes around appointments. This awareness helps you communicate with your partner and care team about what you need.

Create a Keepsake

Imagine giving your child a transcript of your voice journals from their pregnancy. The excitement when you found out. What you felt during each ultrasound. The night before they were born. OffRecord AI Journal creates searchable, exportable transcripts of every entry. It's the most personal baby book possible.

Hands-Free When You Need It

Third trimester: you can barely reach your toes, let alone sit at a desk and write. Voice journaling works from the couch, from bed, from the bathtub. Hands-free. No effort beyond speaking. And everything stays completely private on your device — no cloud, no account, no data collection.

A Weekly Pregnancy Check-In

  • The body: "This week my body feels..." (document the physical experience)
  • The baby: "The baby is..." (capture milestones and movements)
  • The feelings: "Emotionally I'm..." (be honest with yourself)

Two minutes a week. Nine months from now, you'll have a pregnancy diary you'll treasure forever.
